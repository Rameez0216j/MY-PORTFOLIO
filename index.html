About Me

Full Stack and ML Developer

I am an accomplished AIML and Web Developer with expertise in a range of programming languages, including C/C++, JavaScript, Python, and SQL. In the field of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, I have hands-on experience in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Computer Vision. My proficiency extends to Full Stack Development using the MERN stack, and I am well-versed in various tools and platforms, such as AWS, Git, GitHub, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, Power BI, MongoDB, Linux, Ansible, Postman, and JIRA. Additionally, I have worked with frameworks and libraries like OpenCV, Flask, TensorFlow, React.js, Node.js, Bootstrap, and Tailwind CSS.

Throughout my career, I have undertaken impactful projects, including a real-time drowsiness detection model using the YOLOv5 algorithm deployed on AWS EC2 instances, Sudoku Solver integrated with DevOps tools, a People Entry and Exit Tracker using object detection, and a diabetes risk prediction system with Flask. These projects showcase my prowess in both AIML and web development.

My educational background includes pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science (AI and ML) with a stellar 9.27 CGPA from Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering. My commitment to excellence and innovation is underscored by my achievements, including ranking in the top 2% in the Applied Accelerated Artificial Intelligence exam and presenting a research paper on "Helmet-less Bikers Detection" at a National Conference at Andhra University. I am a versatile and well-rounded professional, proficient in English, Hindi, and Telugu, and I thrive as an AIML and Web Developer.
